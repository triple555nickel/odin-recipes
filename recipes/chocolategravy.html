Chocolate Gravy and Southern Biscuits

Southern Style Chocolate Gravy and Biscuits

Here is a southern classic! Many people hase never heard of chocolate gravy and buttermilk biscuits, so I guess you could say it is a diabetic delicacy!

This is one of my favorite things that I eat when I go home to see my family!

Ingredients

  • Chocolate
  • Biscuits
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Make buiscuits
  2. Mix chocolate, butter, milk, and flour together in pot
  3. Heat on medium-high heat for 15-20 minutes until desired consistency
  4. Enjoy!
