Smoked Pork Ribs Recipe!

Smoked Pork Ribs

Battle of BBQ! Many flavors across the south when it comes to good BBQ, but here is my favorite!

Chop up some hickory wood and get that smoker bellowing!

Ingredients

  • Pork ribs
  • BBQ Sauce
  • Salt and Black Pepper
  • Meat tenderizer
  • Marinate for 24 hours

Steps

  1. Place in aluminum foil, but leave top open
  2. Adjust smoker to 275 degrees
  3. Smoke for 8-10 hours
  4. Should be fall off the bone!
