Italian Sausage Pizza Recipe!

Italian Sausage Pizza

One question...New York or Deep Dish? We'll keep it simple for this recipe, but it is still good all the same! Bon Appetite!

Fire up that oven and let's get to slingin' some dough!

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Sausage
  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Spread dough
  2. Spread tomato sauce on dough
  3. Spread cooked sausage and cover with shredded cheese
  4. Bake at 450 degrees for 25 minutes
